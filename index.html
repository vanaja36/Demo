The Problem with Fresh Food

⚠️

Harmful Chemicals

Fruits, vegetables, and other fresh foods often contain harmful chemicals used to enhance freshness or extend shelf life.

🔍

Hidden Dangers

These chemicals can cause severe health problems like toxicity, hormonal imbalance, and long-term diseases.

❓

Lack of Transparency

Consumers have no easy way to identify whether the food they consume is safe or not.

Introducing FreshCheck

📱

Scan Functionality

Use your phone to scan food items for chemical levels with advanced AI technology.

🏥

Health Assessment

Receive personalized recommendations based on your health data and dietary needs.

⚡

Real-Time Results

Get instant feedback on food safety and chemical percentages within seconds.
